## About hover

There's no separate `--hover` variable — the Tailwind/shadcn convention reuses <code style={codeStyle}>accent</code> for hover states (`hover:bg-accent`). The real open item (see the audit's action plan) is confirming that the `accent` value here matches the hover color used in R7 production today.
